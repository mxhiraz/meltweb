Why It Matters

Vented the correct way — every connection, every time.

Done right, a dryer vent should give you shorter drying times immediately and better airflow than before. Done wrong, it becomes a lint trap waiting to ignite. Here's the Almetal standard:

  • No screws or rivets in the airway — nothing for lint to catch on.
  • Metal foil tape on every joint — never duct tape.
  • Minimal 90° turns — efficient, low-resistance airflow.
  • No screen wire at the exit — it blocks air and collects lint.
