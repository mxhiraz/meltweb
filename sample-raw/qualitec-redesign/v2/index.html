Our Mindset

A specialist mindset.

Infrared heating isn't a side line for us — it's the entire focus. Radiant heaters emit infrared energy that converts to heat when it contacts solid objects: floors, walls, equipment, people. The result is faster comfort, lower bills, and systems that hold up to Western Canada's climates.

That focus is what makes our advice useful, our specs accurate, and our 40-year partnership with SRP — Canada's leading infrared manufacturer — possible.
