About Us

We don't sell roofs. We build relationships.

CityScapes Roofing was founded on a simple idea: refocus the industry on the customer. Keith Haugen brings 38 years of San Diego roofing experience, having previously served as superintendent at Witherow Roofing. Co-founder Mark Larson joined to make that vision a reality.

Every installer on our crew carries 10+ years of experience across all roofing material types — shingle, tile, metal, torch-down, and fluid-applied coatings. The result: workmanship that holds up against San Diego rain, sun, and salt air, and an experience that earns referrals.
