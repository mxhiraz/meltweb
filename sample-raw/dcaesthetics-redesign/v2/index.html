Regenerative treatment

Platelet-Rich Fibrin — PRP, improved

PRF isolates plasma, growth factors, white blood cells and fibrin from your own blood, injected into the skin with ultra-fine needles. There is no downtime, and it is a very safe procedure.

Skin texture, wrinkles, dryness, redness, pore size, brown spots and under-eye darkness can all be improved — with significant results seen after three treatments.

Trans & non-binary clients

A relaxed, friendly & non-judgemental space

I offer reduced-cost treatments for trans and non-binary clients — sensitive facial feminisation and masculinisation using dermal fillers, grounded in a knowledge of the classical differences between the ‘masculine’ and ‘feminine’ skull: brow heaviness, cheekbone shape, jaw angle and chin width.

Facial transformation isn’t desired by everyone, and I’m happy to proceed gradually through staged treatments. Consultations are free, longer sessions are always booked out, and complete anonymity is assured.
