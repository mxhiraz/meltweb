Our Process

From inspection to installation, every step is handled in-house

Most residential roofs are completed in 1–2 days. We pull every required permit, coordinate with your insurance for storm claims, and clean the site spotless — so you only experience the result, not the disruption.

  1. 01
    Free Inspection

    HAAG-certified diagnosis of damage, scope, and material recommendations for the North Texas climate.

  2. 02
    Transparent Estimate

    Clear, line-item pricing with financing options subject to credit approval.

  3. 03
    Certified Installation
