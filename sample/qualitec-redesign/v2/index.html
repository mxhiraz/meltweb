Radiant Heating Technology

Heat the Objects, Not the Air

Instead of heating the air, radiant heaters emit infrared energy that's converted into heat when it contacts solid objects like floors, walls and people — for faster, more efficient comfort.

01

Energy Efficiency

Radiant heaters can save up to 50% of energy by directly heating materials instead of air, particularly in large spaces with high air infiltration.

02

Low Maintenance

Minimal maintenance with no need for filter or duct cleaning, and minimal moving parts to service.

03

Cleaner Air with HRV & ERV

GreenTek recovery ventilators improve the air we breathe while increasing efficiency and lowering carbon emissions.
