Understanding Radiant Heating

Heat the objects, not the air.

Instead of heating the air, radiant heaters emit infrared energy that's converted into heat the moment it touches solid objects — floors, walls and people.

Up to 50% energy savings

Radiant heaters can save up to 50% of energy by directly heating materials instead of air — ideal for large spaces with high air infiltration.

Low maintenance

No filter or duct cleaning required, with minimal moving parts to service — so your system keeps running with less downtime.

Our Story

Four decades of bringing the heat to Western Canada.
