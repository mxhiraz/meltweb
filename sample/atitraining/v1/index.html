Why ATI

The right choice to turn your dream into reality

Located in Las Vegas, Nevada, the Advanced Training Institute (ATI) trains students for industry-recognized certifications in high-demand, technical careers. We give you the hands-on training you need to get started today.

Students at ATI come from all walks of life. Some juggle school with family; others hold a job while they attend. That's why ATI offers both day and evening courses — so a motivated person like you can make a new career a reality.
