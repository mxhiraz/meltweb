FAQ

Answers to the questions Sydney homeowners ask us most

Do you really beat any written quote by 10%?

Yes. Send us any legitimate written like-for-like quote — same scope, same materials, same project — and we'll beat it by 10%. It's been our standing offer for years.

How long does a typical re-roof take?

Most residential re-roofs are completed within 7 days. Larger or weather-affected projects may take longer, but we'll always give you an honest timeframe in writing before we start.

What's the difference between Colorbond and Zincalume?

Both are Australian-made steel roofing products. Zincalume® is an unpainted steel coated for corrosion resistance — durable and cost-effective. Colorbond® has the same base plus a baked-on paint finish in 22+ colours. We'll help you choose based on your home, climate and budget.

Can you convert my tile roof to metal?

Absolutely — it's one of our most-requested services. We safely remove your existing tiles, then install lightweight, weather-resistant steel that improves durability, look, and energy efficiency.

Do you provide written warranties?

Yes. We use trusted Australian-made products like Colorbond® and Zincalume® which carry manufacturer warranties up to 25 years. Every install meets strict safety and building codes.

Which suburbs do you cover?

We're Sydney-based and specialise in the Eastern Suburbs and Inner West — including Rosebery, Randwick, Kingsford, Zetland, Eastlakes, Waterloo, Moore Park, Mascot, Botany, Pagewood, Kensington, Redfern and Bondi. Just outside that area? Still call us.
