Frequently Asked

Answers to questions Sydney homeowners ask us most

Quick answers on warranties, materials, timeframes and our beat-any-quote guarantee.

Do you really beat any written quote by 10%?

Yes. Send us any legitimate written like-for-like quote — same scope, same materials, same project — and we'll beat it by 10%. It's been our standing offer for years.

How long does a typical re-roof take?

Most residential re-roofs complete within 7 days. Larger or weather-affected projects may take longer — we'll give you an honest written timeframe before we start.

Colorbond vs Zincalume — what's the difference?

Both are Australian-made. Zincalume® is unpainted steel coated for corrosion resistance — durable and cost-effective. Colorbond® has a baked-on paint finish in 22+ colours. We'll help you choose based on your home, climate and budget.

Can you convert my tile roof to metal?

Absolutely — one of our most-requested services. We safely remove existing tiles and install lightweight, weather-resistant steel that improves durability, look and energy efficiency.

Do you provide written warranties?

Yes. We use trusted Australian-made products like Colorbond® and Zincalume® which carry manufacturer warranties up to 25 years. Every install meets strict safety and building codes.

Which Sydney suburbs do you cover?

We specialise in Eastern Suburbs and Inner West — including Rosebery, Randwick, Kingsford, Zetland, Eastlakes, Waterloo, Moore Park, Mascot, Botany, Pagewood, Kensington, Redfern and Bondi. Outside the area? Still call us.
