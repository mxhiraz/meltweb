Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I consider a full roof replacement?

A roof replacement is necessary if your roof is over 20 years old, has widespread shingle damage, sagging, or persistent leaks.

How do I choose a reliable roofing contractor?

Look for a licensed and insured roofing contractor with verified local references, solid warranties, and transparent estimates.

What should I do after storm damage to my roof?

Contact our storm damage service immediately. We'll perform an inspection and document the damage for insurance.

Is roofing replacement covered by homeowners insurance?

Homeowners insurance may cover roof replacement if the damage is due to covered events like hail, wind, or falling debris.

Do you offer gutter services with roofing projects?

Yes, we offer complete gutter services including installation, repair, and cleaning — often paired with roof upgrades.

Can you install energy-efficient roofing materials?

Yes, we offer a range of energy-efficient roofing options including reflective shingles and insulated roof panels.

How do I maintain my roof to extend its life?

Schedule annual inspections, clean gutters regularly, trim nearby trees, and address small issues like loose shingles immediately.
