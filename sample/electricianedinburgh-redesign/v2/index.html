Consumer units

Fuse board replacements

A consumer unit — also known as a fuse board or electrical fuse box — distributes electricity from the mains supply to individual circuits around your property. We upgrade outdated boards to keep your home safe and compliant.

Rewiring

Full house rewiring

A house rewiring involves changing all the wiring and upgrading the home's electrical equipment. It's recommended that you change all your house wiring every 25 years — we handle the whole job, start to finish.
