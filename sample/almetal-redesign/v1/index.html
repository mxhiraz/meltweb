The Problem We Fixed

The safest, most efficient way to vent your dryer

You clean the lint filter every load — but lint still builds up. It settles inside flexible foil and plastic hoses, where it restricts airflow. That trapped heat is exactly what turns an ordinary dryer into a fire hazard.

Almetal replaces that hose with a precision aluminum periscope that sits flush against the wall, so air moves freely and lint has nowhere to collect.

  • Reduces overheating and dryer-fire risk
  • Dries clothes faster on less energy
  • Lets the dryer sit closer to the wall
  • Pays for itself in energy savings

Installation

Up and running in a few hours

Most installs take a few hours with standard tools. In some homes a contractor is advisable — and we can refer a local HVAC pro.

1

Measure & Set Up

Pull the dryer out, take a few measurements, and position the periscope and receiver behind it.

2

Mount the Receiver

The receiver is installed against the wall, ceiling or floor depending on your vent configuration.

3

Fit the Extender

The exhaust pipe extender is installed directly on the dryer for a clean, sealed connection.

4

Reconnect & Done

Slide the dryer back into place, reconnect to the vent system, and enjoy faster, safer drying.
