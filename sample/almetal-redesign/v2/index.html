About Almetal

We make dryer venting safer & smarter

It has become a personal goal of ours to place an Almetal® Dryer Vent in as many homes as possible. Clothes dryers are among the highest energy-consuming appliances in any home — and the way they vent has a direct impact on both your safety and your power bill.

Our rigid aluminum periscope system replaces crushable foil hoses with a clean, straight airflow path. It pays for itself in a short time through energy savings alone.

Why Use Almetal

The perfect solution for your laundry room

Even with regular filter cleaning, lint accumulates in hoses and restricts airflow. Here's how Almetal keeps your dryer cool, fast and safe.

Safer Against Fire

Trapped lint and restricted airflow cause dryers to overheat — a leading household fire hazard. Almetal's straight aluminum path gives lint nowhere to collect.

More Efficient

Better airflow means clothes dry in a fraction of the time on far less energy — so the kit pays for itself in savings on one of your home's hungriest appliances.

Space-Saving Design

The low-profile periscope lets your dryer sit flush against the wall — no crushed hose, no wasted space, just a clean, professional finish.

Installation

Installed in a few hours

Most installs take just a few hours with standard tools. In some homes a contractor is advisable — and we'll gladly refer a local HVAC pro.

01

Measure & Set Up

Pull the dryer out, take a few measurements, and position the periscope and receiver behind it.

02

Mount Receiver

The receiver is installed against the wall, floor or ceiling to match your vent configuration.

03

Fit Extender

The exhaust pipe extender is installed directly on the dryer for a clean, sealed connection.

04

Reconnect

Slide the dryer back, reconnect to the vent system, and enjoy faster, safer drying.
