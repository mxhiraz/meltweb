About Us

Vented the Correct Way

Almetal Dryer Vent Corporation

Most people don't realize their clothes dryer is one of the highest energy-using appliances in their home. Even with a regularly cleaned lint filter, lint keeps building up inside the hose — restricting airflow, lengthening drying, and creating a fire risk.

It has become a personal goal of ours to place an Almetal® Dryer Vent in as many homes as possible. Vent your clothes dryer the correct way and you'll cut your energy bills, be greener, and make your home safer in the process.

Our dryer vent pays for itself in a short time with the energy savings alone — and we back every install with the best product information and proven installation methods.
