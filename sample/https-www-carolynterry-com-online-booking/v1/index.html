Welcome to the Studio

Where expertise meets calm.

Carolyn Terry is a licensed esthetician in Chicago's Gold Coast with nearly four decades of experience in personalized skin care. Her studio is more than a place to get a facial — it's a private, unhurried space designed around you.

Her approach is planning-based: focused on assessment, consistency, tolerance, and long-term skin health — never quick fixes. Every cut of time is spent reading your skin and tailoring the treatment to where you are today, so results carry between visits.
