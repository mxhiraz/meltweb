About Dr Coventry

Dr Daniel Coventry
BMBCh (Oxon) BSc

“My aim is always for a natural result: building on what the client already has rather than creating a false-looking facade. This approach requires a high level of knowledge of facial anatomy.”

Dr Dan moved to Brighton in 2016 to work as a doctor, later training on Harley Street as an aesthetic practitioner. He has trained alongside plastic, head & neck and ENT surgeons in both the UK and USA.

Trans & non-binary clients

A relaxed, friendly & non-judgemental space

I offer reduced-cost treatments for trans and non-binary clients. Using dermal fillers and a knowledge of the classical differences between the ‘masculine’ and ‘feminine’ skull — brow heaviness, cheekbone shape, jaw angle and chin width — I provide sensitive facial feminisation and masculinisation.

Facial transformation isn’t desired by everyone, and I’m happy to proceed gradually through staged treatments. Consultations are free, and I always book out longer sessions for trans clients for this reason.
