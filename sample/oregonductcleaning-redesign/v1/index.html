About Our Company

Premium service for a remarkably cleaner home

Our powerful HEPA vacuums are remote controlled. We clean from each register toward your furnace — brushing and vacuuming your ducts at the same contact point — breaking up and removing years of accumulated dust and debris from your system.

We pre-vacuum each floor vent with a 20-foot hose to clear large debris, then run motorized spinning brushes through your ductwork for a deep, thorough clean from every angle.

The Benefits

Why a clean duct system matters

Improve air quality

Removing dust, allergens and bacteria means cleaner, healthier air circulating through every room.

Lower energy costs

A clean system breathes freely, so your furnace and HVAC don't have to work as hard to heat and cool.

Reduces dust & bacteria

Less dust settling on surfaces and fewer airborne particles for you and your family to breathe.

Extends HVAC lifespan

Clean ducts reduce strain on your equipment, helping your system last longer and run smoother.

A cleaner home

We're mindful of pets and kids, clean up everywhere we work, and leave your home better than we found it.

Prevents dryer fires

Dryer fires are the #1 cause of home fires. Regular vent cleaning keeps flammable lint from building up.
