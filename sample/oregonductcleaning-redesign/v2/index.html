Our Services

Thorough cleaning, done right

Two specialized services that keep your air clean and your home safe.

Air Duct Cleaning

We pre-vacuum each floor vent with a 20-foot hose, then run motorized spinning brushes on remote-controlled hoses through your ductwork, vacuuming at every contact point.

  • Cleaned register-to-furnace & back
  • Improves air quality & airflow
  • Extends the life of your HVAC system

Dryer Vent Cleaning

Why get your dryer duct cleaned? First, it'll reduce your dry time. More importantly: dryer fires are the #1 cause of fires in homes. We recommend cleaning every 1–2 years.

  • Faster, more efficient drying
  • Removes flammable lint buildup
  • Reduces the risk of a home fire
